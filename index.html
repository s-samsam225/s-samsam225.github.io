Our Mission


At oSTEM, we are dedicated to fostering a supportive and inclusive community for LGBTQ+ individuals in STEM. Through mentorship, professional development, and social events, we empower students to thrive academically, professionally, and personally. Join us in building a future where diversity drives innovation!
